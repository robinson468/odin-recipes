Creamy Macaroni and Cheese

This creamy and delicious dish is loved by kids from age 2 to 92. It works great as a side dish, but we often make it our main course.

Use whole-grain elbow macaroni and organic ingredients if available. It is helpful to make 2 batches at a time because you're going to want to have leftovers!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • one-half cup butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded white Cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices day-old bread, torn into small pieces
  • salt and pepper to taste
